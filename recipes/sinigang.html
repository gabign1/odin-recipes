Sinigang

Dish Description

Sinigang is a sour Fillipino soup primarily flavored with tamarind or other sour fruits. The word Sinigang means "stewed [dish]" in Tagalog, and can refer to any meat or seafood cooked in a sour broth (though this recipe calls for pork).

Ingredients

  • Pork Belly or Ribs
  • Young tamarind
  • Water Spinach
  • String Beans
  • Eggplant
  • Daikon Radish
  • Long green pepper

Steps

  1. Extract the flavors from the tamarind
  2. Boil the tamarind broth and cook the meat
  3. Add the vegetables sequentially
  4. Season and add the remaining ingredients
